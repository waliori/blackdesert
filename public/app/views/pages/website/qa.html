SUBSCRIPTION

How it works

By subscribing to our services,
you will be able to access all the tools of our catalogue available by subscription!

When you subscribe, you will have access to all the advanced features:

  • Time tool for cooking and alchemy: you get notified when your cooking/alchemizing session ends

The subscription is done on a recurring basis.

You can deactivate your subscription at any time, there is no minimum commitment period!


Is it possible to share my account with family and friends?

Access to your subscriber account is limited within the same household and family.

For the moment, we do not allow account sharing between friends in order to cover our expenses (hosting, domain name, mailing service ...).


How do I cancel my subscription? Is the subscription recurring?

When subscribing to a subscription, a recurring payment without obligation is made, it will be renewed on the anniversary date.
